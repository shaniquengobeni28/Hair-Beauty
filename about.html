Our Story

Ney’s Hair and Beauty are a premium boutique based in Polokwane, specializing in high-quality frontal wigs, and natural hair care. Currently, the business relies on word of mouth and social media. This project aims to develop a professional, multi-page website to centralize booking, showcase a high-end hair gallery, and provide clear maintenance information to clients
