    Our Story

    Ney'S Hair and Beauty boutique was established in 2024 by Shanique Ngobeni. After completing her matric in 2025, Shanique recognized that they are young professionals and students who struggle to maintain their natural hair and need high quality frontal hair. It began as a personalized service for family and friends and it quickly evolved into a beauty boutique known for its signature melt and meticulous hair transformation. The premium boutique is now based in Polokwane, specializing in high quality frontal installation and natural hair treatment. Our main purpose is to help young professionals and students gain confidence from our services.
